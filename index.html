a day in my life

A daily mood tracker app that helps you record the quality of your life. With this app, you can easily track your mood on a daily basis and generate weekly, monthly, and yearly stats and graphs to gain insight into your emotional well-being. Features a simple and intuitive interface that allows you to quickly and easily record your daily mood. You can view stats and graphs that show how your mood changes over time, allowing you to identify trends and gain insight into your overall well-being. Whether you're looking to improve your emotional health or simply want to keep a record of your daily mood, this is the perfect app for you!
